Quality Policy

The May Modern Group is fully committed to Quality Assurance in all aspects of Design, Construction, Quality Control, in several types of works in the Commercial, Industrial and Infrastructure projects covered by the Quality Management System.

Our objective is the maintenance of appropriate standards of fitness for use, performance, safety, reliability and whenever possible, to improve these standards in line with the client's expectations.
The quality system is in accordance with the requirements of International standards and will review it periodically for continued suitability. In addition, May Modern Group also sets and review quality objectives.

The May Modern Group makes sure that the quality management systems are understood and practiced by all employees in the everyday performance of their duties, our management takes responsibility to ensure that all employees are provided with equipment and facilities as well as documented procedures to enable them to perform their duties in a reliable and effective manner.
